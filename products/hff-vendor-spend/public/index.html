What the report contains

  • Per vendor: net spend and share of total, gross paid, credits, payment count, smallest / median / largest payment.
  • Cadence: the median gap between that vendor's payments, plus how long it has been quiet relative to its own rhythm.
  • Amount drift: the arithmetic difference between the first and last payment recorded for each vendor.
  • Concentration: top 1 / 3 / 5 / 10 share, HHI on the 0–10,000 scale, and how few vendors make up the first 50% and 80%.
  • Monthly and category rollups, reproduced exactly as your file labelled them.
  • Every row accounted for: anything the engine could not read is listed with its CSV line number and the reason — never silently dropped.
