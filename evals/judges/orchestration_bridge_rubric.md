# Orchestration Bridge Rubric

---

## 1. Quality Criteria
* **Score 5 (Excellent)**: Mission decomposed into atomic tasks; zero redundant task emission; proper adapter matching.
* **Score 3 (Acceptable)**: Mission decomposed; routes to correct models but lacks granular execution metadata.
* **Score 1 (Fail)**: Fails to route heavy tasks to GPU/fallback; misses safety policies.
