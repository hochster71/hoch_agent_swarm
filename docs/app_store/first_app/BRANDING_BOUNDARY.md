# Branding Boundary Policy

Rules to prevent exposure of internal architectures:

## Guidelines
* **No internal branding**: Do not mention "Prompt Brain", "Hoch Agent Swarm", or "HASF".
* **No orchestration terms**: Do not refer to "swarms", "critique loops", or "multi-agent networks".
* **Marketing Names**: The application public listing name must remain strictly "RMF Compliance Review Companion".
* **Private internals**: All engine names must remain hidden.
