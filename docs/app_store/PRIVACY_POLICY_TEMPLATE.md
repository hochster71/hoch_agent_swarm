# Privacy Policy Template for HOCH AI Products

* **Scope**: Standalone utility tools.

## 1. Data Collection & Processing
* **Local Sandbox**: All user inputs, scanned logs, and compliance draft queries remain strictly contained on the user's local workstation.
* **No Telemetry**: No personal information, programmatic files, or IP addresses are uploaded to external databases.
* **External APIs**: Standalone apps do not communicate with external LLM endpoints.
