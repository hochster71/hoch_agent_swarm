# Prompt Control Plane — Goal & Mission

## Mission
To establish a safe, local-first, evidence-driven, human-approved AI operating system that increases validated positive production outcomes across personal life, family support, home operations, business execution, app development, and positive AI-for-humanity projects.

## Scope
The scope encompasses all autonomous and semi-autonomous agent operations within the HOCH Agent Swarm, running across the defined control-plane nodes and worker pools.

## North Star Metric
- **Validated Positive Production Outcomes Per Week**

### Definition of Validated Positive Production Outcome
A completed personal, business, home, hobby, cybersecurity, or software-factory outcome that:
1. Met all defined acceptance criteria
2. Passed QA verification tests
3. Passed cybersecurity/security compliance gates
4. Produced full trace/evidence logs
5. Resolved all high-risk ambiguity
6. Improved quality, speed, safety, learning, family life, business capability, or positive human impact

## Operational Domains
- **Personal Life / Family Support**: Organizing schedules, communications, and preserving family safety.
- **Home Operations**: Dashboarding, tracking home-life events, and managing stationary LAN components.
- **Business Execution & HASF**: Executing software development pipelines inside the Hoch Application Software Factory.
- **Cybersecurity**: Vulnerability monitoring, DAST/SAST analysis, and continuous compliance checks.
- **Hobbies & Humanity**: Empowering positive open-source development, safety templates, and community helpfulness.

## Core Doctrines

### Local-First Doctrine
All model orchestration, routing, prompt library execution, and event log stores operate within the local area network (LAN) boundary to ensure data privacy, confidentiality, and resilience. External connections are treated as untrusted and require strict gateway policing.

### Positive-Human-Impact Doctrine
All workflows must align toward producing helpful, ethical, and human-aligned outcomes. Automation is used to augment human productivity and well-being, never to bypass safety rules or perform unchecked actions.
