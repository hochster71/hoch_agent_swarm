# HOCH Prompt Brain — Target Buyer Profile

## 1. Primary Personas
* **GovCon Cybersecurity Teams**: Under immense pressure to deliver ATO packages for DoD or federal projects quickly.
* **ISSO / ISSM Support Teams**: Spend days reviewing control narrative descriptions and POA&M triages manually.
* **DevSecOps Compliance Teams**: Responsible for mapping pipeline runs to NIST SSDF/STIG guidelines.
* **Small Defense Contractors**: Lack dedicated compliance teams; need automated "pre-flight" checks to pass SCA assessments.

---

## 2. Key Messaging Strategy
* Emphasize **review acceleration** (from days to minutes) and **workflow consistency**.
* Highlight **gap detection** (identifying missing timeframe specs, vague owners, or incomplete logs).
* Reassure buyers that **human-in-the-loop approval remains mandatory** and Prompt Brain does not replace human expert judgment.
