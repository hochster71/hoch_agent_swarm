## 2. Assumptions
- Launch Cost Assumption: $15,000 (development, compute matching, compliance audits).
- Monthly Operating Cost: $1,200 (Stripe fees, local node power, API token costs).
- ARPU assumes a tiered SaaS subscription structure.

## 3. Risks & Validations
