# HASF AI Business Operating Model

This document outlines the governance and operational structure of the HOCH HAS/HASF AI-run organization. Under this model, the organization functions as an autonomous entity driven by specialized AI executive agents, while establishing clear human oversight and approval gates.

## 1. Core Operating Philosophy
- **Founder-Centric Governance**: Michael Hoch serves as the Founder, Owner, and Final Approval Authority. Day-to-day management and operational execution are delegated to an AI leadership team.
- **Autonomous Substrates**: HOCH PODS serve as the secure runtime environment executing specific agent tasks.
- **Audit-Ready Evidence Fabric**: Every business decision, execution step, and financial transaction is logged in the evidence fabric to ensure complete transparency.

## 2. Organization Structure
The organization is divided into specialized divisions led by AI Executives:
1. **Operations**: Led by the AI Chief Operating Officer (HAS Commander), overseeing execution pipelines.
2. **Finance**: Led by the AI Chief Financial Officer (HASF Finance Manager), managing unit economics, revenue analysis, and monetization integration.
3. **Technology & Product**: Guided by the AI Technical Director and AI Product Officer to manage build cycles and feature packaging.
4. **Security & Governance**: Enforced by the AI Security & Compliance Officer and AI QA & Release Authority to ensure strict adherence to zero-trust postures.

## 3. Decision-Making Cadence
- **Operations Briefings**: Operations and compute utilization reports are compiled periodically.
- **Finance Operations Briefs**: Generated regularly to detail Stripe monetization status, ROI scenarios, unit economics, and required human approvals.
- **Human Gated Milestones**: Structural updates, monetization deployments, and high-risk operations are staged as proposals requiring explicit cryptographic/final approval from Michael Hoch.
