# Epic Fury Unit Economics

This document analyzes the unit economics (CAC, LTV, MRR, operating costs) of Epic Fury.

## 1. Key Metrics & Targets

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Targeted at $15.00 per paid user (mainly through organic growth and content-based marketing).
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Estimated at $150.00 assuming average subscription duration of 6 months at realistic ARPU of $24.99.
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 10.0x (highly attractive operating target).
- **Churn Rate Goal**: Under 8% monthly churn.
- **Stripe Transaction Fee**: 2.9% + $0.30 per charge (modeled in net ARPU).
- **Local Host Compute Cost**: ~$0.05 per active pod execution hour (highly efficient local compute substrate).
