# HOCH HASF Soccer Intelligence Platform — Product Model

This document outlines the product strategy, target market, features, and risk guardrails for the **HOCH HASF Soccer Intelligence Platform**.

## 1. Product Hypothesis & Value Proposition
Applying computer vision and AI analytics to youth and amateur soccer videos provides actionable coaching metrics, injury avoidance indicators, and parental insights that traditional scouting lacks.

---

## 2. Target Users
- **Youth Club Coaches**: Looking for tactical movement tracking and drill metrics.
- **Student-Athletes & Players**: Visualizing performance progression and drill libraries.
- **Parents**: Desiring insights into development and active injury prevention metrics.

---

## 3. Revenue & Pricing Model
- **Basic Subscription**: $15/month for individual player analytics.
- **Premium Team Plan**: $45/month for full squad CV spacing analytics.
- **Transactional Plan**: $5 per uploaded match analysis report.

---

## 4. Key Soccer Intelligence Features
- **Computer Vision Mesh**: Tracking player positioning and spacing mesh in local browser Sandbox (CvSandbox).
- **Overuse Prevention Engine**: Automatic check enforcing the **Jayanthi Overuse Rule** (Hours per week of organized sports must not exceed the player's age).
- **Drill Library**: Movement tutorials mapping tactical positioning.

---

## 5. Risk & Compliance Guardrails
- **COPPA & Privacy Zero-Trust Stance**: All raw video frames process locally on device. No video payloads of minors are transferred to cloud databases.
- **Prediction-Risk Disclaimer**: AI prediction metrics are purely for athletic development. Explicit disclaimer states it is not a clinical diagnosis tool.
- **Anti-Gambling Restriction**: Prediction engines are locked; no outcomes may be integrated with betting APIs.
