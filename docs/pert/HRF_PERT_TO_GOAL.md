## The GOAL of HRF (north star)

> **HRF autonomously generates, prioritizes, and advances research moonshots across humanity's
> grand-challenge domains — producing novel, literature-grounded, citation-VERIFIED, reproducible
> research outputs (hypotheses → syntheses → experiment designs → computational findings). Every
> claim resolves to a real primary source (PubMed / bioRxiv / ClinicalTrials / ChEMBL); no
> fabricated citations, no invented results, no fake-green.**

Research's deadly failure mode is *hallucinated citations and fabricated findings*. So HRF's defining
gate is **citation verification** — the anti-hallucination analogue of HMF's originality gate — and
you already have the tools wired to enforce it.
