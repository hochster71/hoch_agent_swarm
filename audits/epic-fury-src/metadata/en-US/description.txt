BUILT BY A COMBAT-SEASONED NAVAL OFFICER. POWERED BY AI.

Epic Fury 2026 is the only real-time military intelligence dashboard built by someone who's actually operated in the waters, theaters, and threat environments it covers.

Michael Hoch served 31 years as a U.S. Navy Mustang — enlisted to officer — retiring as a Lieutenant Commander (O-4), Surface Warfare Officer and Limited Duty Officer in Surface Operations. From the Persian Gulf to the Red Sea, from 1988 to 2019, he lived the operational realities that this app tracks in real time.

This isn't aggregated headlines. This is pattern-of-life analysis, theater situational awareness, and AI-synthesized intelligence — the way a watch officer would want it.
